Category: (1) TAM Application Type

Application Identifier: 3.10

Maturity Level: 4

Overview

Solution Management applications provide the necessary functionality to manage the analyzing, composing, and recommending of the right solution to a given potential customer.

Functionality

Solution Management provides management of the sales solution from capturing the requirements through the initial designing of the solution, initial feasibility assessment, pricing of the solution using both tariff and custom prices until (preferably) order request generation. Solution management functionality includes:

  • Solution requirement capture
  • Solution design
  • Solution pricing
  • Proposal management
  • Price Optimization
  • Offer Management
  • Sales Negotiation
