Description

Close a revenue assurance trouble report when the issues leading to the revenue leakage have been resolved.

Extended Description

The objective of the Close Revenue Assurance Trouble Report processes is to close a revenue assurance trouble report when the issues leading to the revenue leakage have been resolved. These processes monitor the status of all open revenue assurance trouble reports, and recognize that a revenue assurance trouble report is ready to be closed when the status is changed to cleared.
