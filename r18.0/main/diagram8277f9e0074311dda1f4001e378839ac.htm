4. Product Management Domain

Diagram Description

Product Management is the organization’s approach to the process of developing, managing and marketing its products offerings to the customer. Product Management is about identifying what products to sell, what they are comprised of, who they are sold to, how they are sold, supported and serviced, how they perform in the market and how they are managed through to retirement. Product Management contains four key facets:

  • Product Strategy / Proposition Management
  • Product Catalog Management
  • Product Lifecycle Management
  • Product Performance Management
