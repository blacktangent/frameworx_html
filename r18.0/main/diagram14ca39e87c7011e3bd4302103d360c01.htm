1.7.3.2 Enterprise Quality Management

Diagram Description

Enterprise Quality Management processes define the enterprise quality management policies and the enterprise model for quality management. They support all operations and lifecycle processes in the implementation and control of this model.
Note that quality is actually assessed within each area of the enterprise, but follows the guidelines defined by this process, which provides a template for specific applications of quality management in any areas of the Enterprise
