9.1 Revenue Assurance Management

Diagram Description

Revenue Assurance is the collection of data quality and process improvement methods that reduce leakages, improve profits, revenues and cash flows without influencing demand.

Applications in this functional area vary considerably and they relate to almost any area of the OSS/BSS applications. The revenue assurance applications area is thus shown in this part of the TMF Applications Map for convenience.

Although, as its name suggest, Revenue Assurance is mainly about revenues and profits, revenue assurance activities in the Telco organization has significant implications on operational and strategic aspects of the service provider.
