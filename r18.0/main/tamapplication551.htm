Overview

Solution Requirement Capture application enables the capture of customer solution requirement in the pre-sale phase.

Functionality

  •  Overall Solution Requirement Capture – Capture the overall solution requirements (e.g. target date, customer locations, multi-location/shared services)
  • Location Requirement Capture – Capture the particular requirements for each customer location as part of the desired solution
