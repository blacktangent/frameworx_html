Frameworx Domain: Supplier_Partner Domain

  • Description
  • Associations

The Supplier/Partner domain encompasses planning of strategies for Supplier/Partners, handling of all types of contact with the Supplier/Partner, the management of the relationship, and the administration of Supplier/Partner data. It also supports bills, disputes and inquiries associated with a Supplier/Partner. While both Supplier and Partner are distinct third parties assisting with meeting an Enterprise's customer needs, they are different in the business model and risk sharing in the value chain under consideration. Partners have a greater risk share in an end customer transaction. Supplier/Partner is framed in Application Framework by the Supplier/Partner Domain. With the necessary removal of the Supplier/Partner Domain in the Application Framework in the future, the current definition in this Frameworx Concepts and Principles document will be removed as well.
