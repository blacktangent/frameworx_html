1.6.12.7.1 Receive Party Bill Inquiry

Diagram Description

Receive Party Bill Inquiry creates a party bill inquiry. A party bill inquiry may be created as a result of a specific party initiated bill inquiry or complaint. It may also request and receive additional information from a party if needed to further process the inquiry.
