Frameworx Application: Workforce Configuration and Setup

  • Description
  • Associations

Category: (2) TAM Application Type

Application Identifier: 7.12.5

Maturity Level: 4

Overview

Workforce Configuration and Setup applications provide the necessary functionality to appropriately configure the Workforce Management application, including the setup of working areas, staff information, activity types and related information, etc.

Functionality

Workforce Configuration and Setup applications functionality includes:

  • Classification of different types of manual activities
  • Management of the various geographical working areas (turfs).
  • Management of the dispatchable technicians, including skill sets.
  • Management of technician training and/or coordination and interface with HR systems regarding the training.
  • Management of staff availability, including vacation time.
  • Configuration of the appointment schedule.
  • Forecasting the workload and respective assignable staffing requirements on a daily, weekly, and longer period basis.
