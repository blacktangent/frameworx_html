Frameworx Application: Supply Chain Management

  • Description
  • Associations

Category: (1) TAM Application Type

Application Identifier: 9.10

Maturity Level: 4

Overview

Supply Chain Management provides the necessary functionality to support the planning of needed materials and services, procurement of these materials and services, stocking and shipping preparation, and transportation to their final destination.

Functionality

Supply Chain Management functionality includes:

  • planning of materials based on consumption, marketing plans, re-stocking, and location,
  • requisition management, purchase orders, inventory management, and invoicing for payment,
  • logistics functions of receiving materials, put away, distribution orders, picking, packing, and labeling for these orders,
  •  transportation planning and shipping of materials to their final destination,
  • tracking of materials shipped to and receipted at their final destination.
