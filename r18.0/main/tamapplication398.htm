  • Description
  • Associations

Category: (2) TAM Application Type

Application Identifier: 5.1.1

Maturity Level: 4

Overview

Customer Hierarchy & Group Management provides the necessary functionality to manage the customer hierarchy for both business and residential type customers.

Functionality

Customer Hierarchy & Group Management functionality includes:

  • Customer organizational hierarchy (relevant for household, business and corporate customers)
  • Customer roles (husband, wife, child, user, payer)
  • Customer data matching - deterministic and heuristic algorithms for matching customer data coming from multiple sources
  • Duplication handling -  identify and manage suspected duplication
  • Relationships between Customer instances and/or other data entities such as party, customer bill, customer order, address, or customer problem reports.
