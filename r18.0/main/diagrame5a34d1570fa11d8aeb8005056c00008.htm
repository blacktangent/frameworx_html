Diagram Description

The Operations (OPS) process area contains the direct operations vertical end-end process groupings of Fulfillment, Assurance & Billing (the FAB process groupings), together with the Operations Support & Readiness process grouping. The FAB process groupings are sometimes referred to as Customer Operations processes.
