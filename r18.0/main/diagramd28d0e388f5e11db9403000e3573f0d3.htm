9. Enterprise Domain

Diagram Description

Introduction

Enterprises need to manage themselves and the Application Map has identified the following application areas:

  • Revenue Assurance Management
  • HR Management
  • Financial Management
  • Asset Management
  • Security management
  • Knowledge Management
  • Fraud Management
  • Regulatory & Compliance Management
  • Supply Chain Management

Mostly these functions are quite generic across many industry sectors and are not peculiar to the Communication industry.

Within Knowledge Management the communications industry has some very specific needs around the general title of ‘Enterprise Information Applications or ‘Business intelligence’.
